01 / BRAND PLATFORM

Identity & Design Principles

Ochi Jewelry stands for luxury, heritage, and precision. Our visual identity balances classic elegance (traditional serifs, deep obsidian backgrounds) with modern interactions (subtle micro-animations, glassmorphism, responsive grids).

03 / TYPOGRAPHY

Font System

Our pairing combines classic editorial elegance with modern digital legibility.

Display Face (Cormorant Garamond)
Aa Bb Cc Dd Ee Ff Gg Hh Ii Jj Kk Ll Mm Nn Oo Pp Qq Rr Ss Tt Uu Vv Ww Xx Yy Zz

Cormorant Garamond

Used primarily for hero banners, page headers, section titles, and key branding items. Regulates spacing, regality, and traditional luxury.

h1 Hero Headline (64px)

h2 Section Title (40px)

Body Face (Inter)
Aa Bb Cc Dd Ee Ff Gg Hh Ii Jj Kk Ll Mm Nn Oo Pp Qq Rr Ss Tt Uu Vv Ww Xx Yy Zz

Inter Sans

Used for descriptions, body text, navigation bars, buttons, form controls, and metadata. Highly legible on all digital viewports, particularly at small scale.

Lead Paragraph (18px) — Used for sub-headers and summaries.

Body Copy (15px) — Standard text block size. Written in Inter, regular weight, with 1.6-1.8 line height for optimal legibility.

Small / Caption Copy (12px) — Used for section labels, timestamps, and badges.
